About Me

Hi, I'm Vanessa!

Hey! I'm the creator of Get Hooked Crochet Creations. I'm completely self-taught and picked up crocheting as a hobby while going through school and quickly got hooked! What started as making stuffies for friends and family turned into something I knew I had to share. I love making stuffies because they're so fun to create, and nothing beats seeing someone's face light up when they get one.

Everything I make is fully customizable. Whether it's the yarn colour, yarn type, or little accessories. I'm still learning and always eager to try new things, so if you have something in mind, I'm more than willing to give it a go to make your creations exactly what you imagined.

The animals I have listed are just the beginning of what I can do, so if you have an idea for a creation that isn't already on here, I'd love to chat and figure out how to bring your vision to life.

What Goes Into Every Creation

Giving Back

Crocheting With Purpose

I've always had a huge love for animals, which is one of the reasons I enjoy making stuffed animals so much. Because of that love, I wanted Get Hooked Crochet Creations to give back in a meaningful way.

A portion of every purchase will be donated to the Quinte Humane Society to help support animals in need. Every stuffy you order not only allows me to create something special and handmade for you, but also helps support a cause that means so much to me.
